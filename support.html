Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install TarsX?

+

Download the installer from our Downloads page and follow the on-screen instructions. The installation process is straightforward and typically takes just a few minutes.

What are the system requirements?

+

TarsX requires Windows 10/11, macOS 11+, or Linux (Ubuntu 20.04+). You'll need at least 4GB of RAM (8GB recommended) and 500MB of free disk space.

Is TarsX free to use?

+

Yes, TarsX is free to download and use. We offer both free and premium versions with additional features available in the premium tier.

How do I report a bug?

+

You can report bugs through our Contact page or by emailing support@tarsx.com. Please include as much detail as possible about the issue you're experiencing.

Where can I find documentation?

+

Comprehensive documentation is available in the application's help menu, or you can visit our documentation portal online. Links are provided in the application.

How often are updates released?

+

We release regular updates with bug fixes and new features. Major updates are typically released monthly, with minor updates and patches as needed.
