+ My Mission

II My mission is to blur the line between imagination and innovation—to prove that the ideas we dream about can be built with passion, curiosity, and persistence. I want to inspire others to learn by doing, to take risks, to fail and rebuild, and to see technology not just as code or circuits, but as a way to bring imagination to life. It isn't just a robot; it's a statement that the future belongs to those who create it—one bold idea, one crazy build, and one spark of curiosity at a time.
